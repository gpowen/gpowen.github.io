What are the differences among all these formats?

Theyre different image formats. PNG files are commonly used on websites and are alternatives to JPGs. PDF files emulate a printed document. SVG images can be compressed or stretched without loss of quality, which isnt a feature in other formats.

What is the difference between barchart and histogram?

Bar charts typically compare (multiple) variables while histograms depict distributions of any particular (single) variable.
